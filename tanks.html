MBT Arjun Tank

Main Battle Tank (MBT) Arjun is a multi-laboratory programme of DRDO with CVRDE as the lead Laboratory. It is a state-of-the-art tank with superior fire power, high mobility, and excellent protection. Twelve Mk 1 prototypes of MBT Arjun have been manufactured and their performance tests have provided satisfactory results.

T90 Bhishma

The T-90S is the latest development in the T-series of Russian tanks and offers increase in firepower, mobility and protection.It is manufactured by Uralvagonzavod in Nizhny Tagil, Russia. It entered service with the Russian Army in 1992.
